Welcome to the Cybernet Archive

This website explores how the **1990s internet transformed from a decentralized, grassroots, creative network into a rapidly commercializing digital frontier**. Themes include the rise of **online shopping**, the early **web advertising economy**, and how these developments reshaped online **culture, identity, and user behavior**.

Project Premise

In the early 1990s, the web was chaotic, experimental, and non-commercial. By the decade’s end, major corporations were reshaping the online landscape with e-commerce platforms, banner ads, tracking technologies, and monetized user behavior.
